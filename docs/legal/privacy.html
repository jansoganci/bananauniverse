Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2026

Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality:

  • Email Address: For account creation and authentication.
  • User ID: For account management.
  • Photos: For AI processing and enhancement.
  • Purchase History: For subscription management.

AI Image Processing

IMPORTANT: Flario uses third-party AI services (including fal.ai) to process your images. When you use AI features, your photos are transmitted securely to external services for processing.

  • Data Transmitted: Uploaded photos are sent to AI servers.
  • Retention: Photos are stored temporarily (typically 24-48 hours) for processing purposes.
  • Privacy: We respect your privacy and do not sell or share your data with unauthorized third parties.

Data Retention

  • Uploaded images: Deleted after temporary processing.
  • Processed results: Stored for 30 days to allow user download.
  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account.

Your Rights

You have the right to view, export, or delete your data at any time. Account deletion can be performed directly within the Flario app settings.
