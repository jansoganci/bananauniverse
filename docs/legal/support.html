Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Flario?

  1. Open the Flario app.
  2. Select a photo from your library.
  3. Choose an AI tool (Background Removal, Headshot, etc.).
  4. Wait for processing to complete.
  5. Save or share your enhanced photo.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. Find Flario in the list and select "Cancel Subscription".

How does the credit system work?

  • New users start with 10 free credits.
  • Each AI operation consumes 1 credit.
  • Credits never expire and stay with your account.
