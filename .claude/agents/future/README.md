# Future Agents - Use After Launch

These agents are **NOT needed right now** but will be useful **after your app launches** and you have real users.

## When to Use These

### 📈 growth-hacker.md
**Use when:** You have 1,000+ users and want to optimize growth

**Good for:**
- Creating referral programs ("Share and get 5 free credits")
- A/B testing paywall prices ($2.99 vs $4.99)
- Building viral loops into the app
- Analyzing retention (why users stop using the app)
- Optimizing conversion rates (more users buying credits)

**Wait until:** 3-6 months after App Store launch

---

### 📊 business-analyst.md
**Use when:** You're raising investment or hiring a team

**Good for:**
- Creating formal Business Requirements Documents (BRDs)
- Process optimization for teams
- Stakeholder management
- ROI calculations for investors
- Gap analysis and reporting

**Wait until:** You have funding or 3+ team members

---

### 🎯 product-manager.md
**Use when:** You need to manage a product roadmap for a team

**Good for:**
- Quarterly OKR planning
- Cross-team coordination
- Formal roadmap planning
- Stakeholder alignment
- Enterprise product management

**Wait until:** You have a product team (5+ people)

---

## What to Use RIGHT NOW Instead
